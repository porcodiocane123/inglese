Introduction

The 80s are full of discoveries and new technologies improve people's lives more and more. One of the symbolic objects of this decade is the video recorder which, with its audio quality, was desired by everyone.

The Evolution of The Video Recorder

The video recorder has been widely used at the time and therefore the idea of ​​renewing it has always been highly appreciated by everyone. In fact, over time it has changed becoming the classic DVD player first, and changing more and more until it is inserted into computers or game consoles. The latest evolution reached was the ability to download movies online or watch them through specific apps, certainly facilitating people and reducing costs.

Type of Technology

The video recorder was a videotape player, which each could contain more or less a 25-minute movie, which currently corresponds to 7GB, in 850x630 resolution which would now result in a bad resolution. Subsequently the HD resolution was introduced which is equivalent to 1280 * 720, which is nowadays an acceptable solution.

Dimension

The video recorder was more or less 45 centimeters long and 20 centimeters wide, but starting from 1982 the dimensions were reduced by about 30% for the new standart just introduced, consequently the videotape size was also halved half,his measurements were about 20 centimeters in length and 15 in width.

Date of Invention

First video recorder was seen around 50’s. over decade , it enter for only commercial use in 1970’s,but only in 1980’s became popular. in addition Philips Company created an Amateur version for those wanted to homemade record.

Purpose of Use

There was primarily 2 types of video recorder:

  • Commercial one, called also Professional video recorder, used for recording tv programs and it could record high video quality, so used for a professional environment
  • Amateur one, used for home recording.
    it obviously cost lower than professional video recorder, and it could record video with average quality.

Price and Popularity

The video recorder can be defined as one of the symbolic elements of the 80s. It was very important because it was one of the first forms of watching films even from home. It was very convenient because it avoided additional costs and was a valid alternative to cinema. In the 1980s, moreover, the sound quality was improved, this increasing its popularity. Its prices, given the times, were acceptable: they varied between 50 and 80 euros which, if we want to be precise, corresponded to 96813/154901 lire.

Age of Those Use it and Time it Was Used

The video recorder was used by people of all ages and in many situations, my parents told me that they used it both at home during their free time, and at school to watch presentations or movies.

Color

The video recorders were black, due to the influence it had on the market of the time.

Intervew

Here is a short but intense interview with a 50-year-old man who spent his young age with this wonderful object.

  • How old were you when this item arrived?
    I remember waiting for him for several years. It had come when I was about 20 years old.
  • What was it for and how long did you spend with it?
    I mainly used it to watch movies on video tapes on VHS. During the week, of course there was school, but on the weekend I didn't miss the opportunity to watch films. During the weekend, in fact, I spent between 2 and 3 hours.
  • Why is it so remembered?
    Mainly because it was a novelty of the time because with it you could watch movies without going to the cinema.
  • Where did you get all the videotapes from?
    I took them from a company called "blockbuster". With them, it was possible to obtain the rental of videotapes for VCR. This was very convenient because it cost much less.
